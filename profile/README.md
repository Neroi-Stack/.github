# Neroi-Stack

Neroi-Stack is an organization dedicated to research and development in the field of AI agent usage. Our mission is to explore innovative applications and frameworks where AI agents can be implemented to solve real-world problems, especially in interactive, collaborative, and gaming contexts.

## Projects

- [MCP-TRPG-Game](https://github.com/Neroi-Stack/MCP-TRPG-Game): An AI-driven tabletop role-playing game project, integrating intelligent agents for narrative and gameplay innovation.
- [NeroiStack](https://github.com/Neroi-Stack/NeroiStack): The foundational research and infrastructure layer for advanced AI agent experimentation.

We are always open to collaboration and new ideas in AI agent research. Feel free to explore our projects or reach out for potential collaboration!
